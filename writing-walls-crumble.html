Walls Crumble

Short poem

poemreflection

⚠ Graphic themes — brief reference to gun violence

The piece

Full text

Walls crumble as I mumble “What have I done?” The ring from a gun no longer can I run As I accept the situation I forced upon myself I was warned Yet now I am forlorn Over the choices I wish I had not made.
