01 · Overview

Overview

This power-amp was heavily based off of Rod Elliot's P217 amplifier design, but instead i hand measured the Vbe values of my transistors, measured the drops of my diodes, and kinda just... threw stuff together that made it respond better. I built this for my final project in ECE 3201 in Spring 2026. This was a great experience helping me understand how amplifiers work, and I look forward to building another amplifier, hopefully on my own next time.

Rod Elliot is actually goated (at way more than just audio too!) — please check him out at Elliot Sound Products
